Ask an Expert
What documents are required for company registration?
Typically you'll need PAN cards and Aadhaar cards of all directors, passport-size photos, address proof, and a registered office address proof. Our team will send you a complete checklist once you initiate the process.
How long does the GST registration process take?
GST registration typically takes 3–7 working days from the date of document submission, subject to government processing times. We ensure your application is error-free to avoid any rejections or delays.
Is the entire process handled online?
Yes, absolutely. From document submission to filing and certificate delivery — everything happens digitally. You never need to visit any government office or our premises.
Do I really need a CA for ITR filing?
While individuals with simple income can self-file, a CA ensures maximum deductions are claimed legally, errors are avoided, and any notices from the department are handled professionally. It's a small cost for long-term peace of mind.
